About Me

I come from a family with academic background. My father was a PhD student in Manchester, UK back in the 90s, so I had the privilege of having access to a computer at an early age. I wrote my first software using C++ back in 1999. I assume my future path and career in the startup and tech ecosystem wasn't coincidental after all. I studied IT Engineering both for my bachelors and masters and got to work for a couple of years with C#, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. Later, I lost my enthusiasm for code and probably for the same reason I chose a management path down the line. I liked the people side more honestly! At 21, I had my first try at building a business with a group of university friends. It led to a website for swapping university books. It eventually became my first failure! Summer 2015, I accidently got introduced to individuals who later became my co-founders down the line. They owned a travel agency and intended to provide tourism services online. Having no sufficient capital, I made a calculated risk and decided to hop on free of charge, and built the first working version in 2 months with the help of a university friend Reza Karimi (Thanks bro!) in return for a share in the travel agency. I coded the front-end with bootstrap and JavaScript and it went live, showed massive traction, and Vistavel was born! Vistavel went to become one of the top choices for users seeking travel insurance, luxury travel services, and VIP bus tickets reaching top 5 sellers for the well-known suppliers of our services. My Second experience with building a startup, hiring, bootstrapping, managing and overcoming hurdles in Vistavel ended upon my exit back in 2019. The startup failed to scale after my exit due to lack of raising sufficient funds on time! Nevertheless, it had a significant impact on my future path leading to product management, and executive roles in well-known startups in Iran later down the road. I've experienced various industries including: - Tourism, - Ride-hailing, - Fintech, - EdTech - FMCG I can help startups prepare better for scale up!
